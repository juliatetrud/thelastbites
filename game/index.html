About The Last Bites

Pip is a young ghost on a haunted cruise ship.

He doesn’t quite remember how he got there, or why his grandmother is on the deck below crying. He doesn’t yet know that the ship — the Mnemosyne — travels between worlds, that the chef speaks to him in a kitchen that smells like his childhood, that every port hides a kind of monster that food, of all things, can soothe.

The Last Bites is a gentle ghost story about grief, memory, and the meals that carry us through both. Each chapter visits a real place — Brooklyn, Tallinn, Southampton, Türkiye, Cape Town, Indonesia, Brazil — and explores a regional food and a regional folklore. Each chapter unlocks a recipe on Julia’s website, so you can cook what Pip tasted.

It’s a small game. It will take a few evenings. It is not scary; it is not cruel. There are no fail states and no combat. Just a boy with a fork, learning what a fork is for.
