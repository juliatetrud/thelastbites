▲ Designer Notes — hover or click

v3 — moonlit-blue palette locked. The cottage stands in cool moonlit blue. Warmth — the lit doors, the sparkles, the figures inside — is rare and meaningful. Pip floats translucent at the foreground; he's cool against the cool but his white-blue is brighter than the room. Per 03-art-and-aesthetic.md's Universal & Regional Palette section.

Mechanics unchanged from v2: click each door to open it, read the offer, close it, advance to summary, choose. Wrong choice → devil reveal → reset to doors. Right choice (door 2) → cat reveal → Haldjas resolution.

What I want you to feel: the warmth of the doors should now mean something. Each door is a small warm pool in a cold room. The right door — the old woman — is the warmth that asks for nothing. Give without getting.
